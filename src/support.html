Free for you, not for us

We love podcasting; some of our shows have been going for over 4 years now, and we love them, but they cost money. There are hosting fees for the podcasts, our websites, and equipment. Our podcasts have alway been ad free and we'd like to keep them that way while still having it be affordable for us to keep doing our shows and make new ones! You can help with this by supporting us via one of the methods below.

Amazon

Capitalism to the Rescue

You're a person who is presumably alive and exists in this modern age. This means you buy things from Amazon. If you use our affiliate link below, you go the same old Amazon you'd expect, things don't cost any extra, but we get a small percentage of the money you spend! No one knows why, but it's a big help to us!

Note that the referral doesn't apply to things you already had in your cart before you clicked the link.

Give to Us and We'll Give Back

This is probably the number one way to support the network and we'll give you stuff in return!

  • We give you stuff (extra episodes, access to a community chat, backer exclusive shows) based on the amount that you give
  • The shows we already do cost us less (or nothing!) and we can afford to make you more podcasts.
